# Image Generation Prompts

Concept visualization only; not fabrication authority.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic studio product photo of a hand-cranked barrel organ planning prototype, with a visible pinned barrel as the mechanical score, crank handle, follower row, tracker levers, pallet box, bellows and small reservoir, serviceable windchest, and a compact rank of flue or reed organ pipes in an open inspection case. The case should look like a review mule rather than finished furniture, with accessible panels and visible action paths. Clean neutral studio background, soft directional lighting, 3/4 product-photography angle, approximately 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic workshop scene of the barrel organ mechanism mule being hand-cranked, with the barrel turning, one follower lifting a tracker, a pallet opening in the windchest, and bellows linkage moving separately for diagnosis. Natural light, small pipe-rank test channel nearby, tools and paper tune sketches on the bench, no fake tune titles or brand marks.

## Detail Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the barrel program and action path: a small pin on the rotating barrel lifting a polished follower, the follower connected to a tracker lever that opens a pallet, with gasketed windchest edge and pipe foot blurred behind. Shallow depth of field, high detail, realistic wood, brass pin, felt, and leather surfaces.

## Negative / Avoid

Avoid text, watermarks, logos, impossible pin spacing, hidden inaccessible action, player-piano paper rolls, extra limbs, distorted hands, and fake measured pipe labels.
